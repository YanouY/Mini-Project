How Cancer Arises?

When I was a little girl, I always heard people who living nearby power plants complain about their daily fear of getting cancer in the future. In those people's view, power plants will influence the environment and finally influence their life in some way. Honestly, at first, I thought it is really an insightful thought and suggested them to move around. However, with more and more power plants embedded in our daily life, I started to doubt my previous thought.

So, will power plants play a role in increasing cancer rate? This project will focus on this question.

The structure of the project is as follows:

  • US Cancer Cases Trends Geographical Distribution Overview
  • Power Plants Georgraphical Distribution in Texas
  • Hypothesis Testing for Cancer Cases Number and Power Plants Capacity
  • Ohio Testing the Conclusion We Made

US Cancer Cases Trends Geographical Distribution Overview

5-Year Average Incidence Rate by States

In the beginning, taking a glance at the geographical distribution of the recent 5 years average increasing rate might inspire us.

The map shows each state average cancer increasing rate. The table listed on the left is all the states whose trend is increasing in recent years and the cubes'size represents the magnitude of overall cancer rate.

There are two states which should be paid more attention, Texas, and Ohio. As the state with the highest cancer rate, it seems like Texas doesn't solve the problem efficiently. It still shows a growing trend. Ohio, on the other hand, has the fastest increasing speed, even though its cancer rate is not so high as Texas.

Do local power plants play an important role in these situations?

Texas - Power Plants Geographical Distribution Exploration

Power Plants Distribution

As we have discussed above, Texas is a very important state, because it has the highest cancer rate and the trend is still increasing in the meanwhile.

We can find a very interesting fact from the charts!

That is, the county's cancer rate(cancer cases per 100000 person) is tightly correlated with local power plant capacity! The top three highest power plants' capacity counties are exactly the top three highest cancer rate counties. Top power plants' capacity counties always have a place on the top cancer rate list in Texas. Besides, we also can conjuncture that residents and government have already realized this problem and are trying to fix this problem because those counties all show a sharp decreasing trend in recent years.

What's the statistical relationship between electricity plant capacity and cancer rate?

Texas - Statistical Relationship Exploration

Hypothesis Testing

The above analysis indicates that nearby power plants capacity might have relationship with the local cancer rate.

Null Hypothesis: There is no inner relationship between the local cancer rate and the electricity generation capacity.

Alternate hypothesis: Local cancer rate and the electricity generation capacity are positively correlated.

What's the statistical relationship between plants capacity and cancer rate?

Eventhough the R-square is extremely small, the P-value is smaller than the significant level and the coefficient is positive as well. The null hypothesis was rejected.

It's not hard for us to reach the conclusion that local power plants indeed have a negative influence in inducing cancer.

Ohio - Case Analysis

As the analysis in US cancer cases distribution overview part, Ohio has the fastest increasing speed and a relatively large population of cancer patients. It's also very meaningful for us to have deeper analysis.

It's excited that we can find some really interesting facts which can prove our previous conclusion!

The data of Gallia county is very meaningful to us. We can see that during 2012-2016, the cancer rate in Gallia county is not shocking, in some extend it's even normal. With recent capacity sharply increasing, Gallia rapidly becomes the most "powerful" city in Ohio, however, at the same time, its cancer incidence rate also reaches the highest.

In the first geographical graph, the Gallia county block is a normal red-brown block as others. However, in the second and third graphs, Gallia becomes more and more distinguishing, especially the third graph, where Gallia is the only green block.

Conclusion:

Living around power plants is not a big deal, but you should keep an eye on if they have increased their capacity! ;)
